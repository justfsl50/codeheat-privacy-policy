CodeHeat ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the CodeHeat mobile application.

By using CodeHeat, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

a. Public Profile Information

CodeHeat may collect publicly available information from coding platforms when you provide your username, including:

  • GitHub username and public contribution data
  • LeetCode username and public submission statistics
  • Coding activity and contribution history
  • Public profile metadata
Important: CodeHeat only accesses publicly available information and does NOT access private data, passwords, or credentials.

b. Device and App Information

We may collect limited device-related information, including:

  • Device model and type
  • Operating system version
  • App version
  • Anonymous performance and crash data

This information helps us improve the app's stability and performance.

c. Local Storage Data

CodeHeat may store locally on your device:

  • Generated heatmap wallpapers
  • User preferences and settings
  • Cached coding activity data

This data remains on your device unless explicitly synced.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Generate coding heatmap wallpapers
  • Display coding activity statistics
  • Improve app performance and reliability
  • Fix bugs and improve user experience
  • Provide core app functionality
We do NOT sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

3. Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information:

  • Most data is stored locally on your device
  • We do not store sensitive personal information on our servers
  • We use secure communication methods when accessing APIs

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.
